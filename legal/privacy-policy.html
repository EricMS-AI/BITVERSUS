Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information BitVersus collects, how it's used, and the choices you have — including how third-party advertising partners such as Google use cookies on this site.

Effective date: July 17, 2026 Applies to: bitversus1.com

1. Overview

BitVersus ("we," "us," or "this site") publishes independent, educational content about student loan forgiveness, refinancing, and repayment. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit bitversus1.com, use our calculators, or contact us. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: information you provide directly, and information collected automatically as you browse.

  • Information you provide: If you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address, selected topic, and message. If you subscribe to updates, we collect your email address.
  • Information collected automatically: Like most websites, we automatically collect certain technical data through cookies and similar technologies, including your IP address, browser type and version, device type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and time spent on the site.
  • Calculator inputs: Figures you enter into our refinance or payment calculators (such as loan balance, interest rate, or term) are processed locally in your browser to generate estimates. We do not store these inputs on our servers or associate them with your identity.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites function properly and allow us — and our advertising and analytics partners — to recognize your browser across visits. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for core site functionality, such as remembering your cookie consent choice.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site (e.g., which pages are most popular) so we can improve content and navigation.
  • Advertising cookies: Used by us and by third-party advertising vendors, including Google, to deliver relevant ads and measure ad performance, as described in detail below.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies may affect how parts of the site function, including our calculators and embedded advertising.

4. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

This site displays advertising served by Google AdSense and may work with other third-party advertising vendors. The following disclosures are required by Google's AdSense program policies and apply to visitors of this site:

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.
  • For more information on how Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services and Google's Advertising Policies.

Note: Advertising vendors, including Google, may combine information collected on this site with information collected elsewhere to serve you personalized ads. We do not control the cookies set by third-party advertisers and recommend reviewing each vendor's own privacy policy for details on their data practices.

5. Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand aggregate site usage. These services use cookies to collect standard log information and visitor behavior data. This information is processed in a way that does not directly identify anyone, and we do not attempt to re-identify visitors from this data.

6. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to messages submitted through our contact form.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the site and its content.
  • To understand aggregate visitor trends and site performance.
  • To display relevant advertising through Google AdSense and other partners.
  • To comply with legal obligations, where applicable.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use contact-form submissions for any purpose beyond responding to your inquiry, unless you separately opt in to receive updates from us.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share limited data with:

  • Advertising partners (such as Google AdSense), for the purposes described in Section 4.
  • Analytics providers, to help us understand site usage.
  • Service providers who help us operate the site (such as hosting or email delivery), under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal authorities, if required to comply with a legal obligation, court order, or to protect our rights.

We do not knowingly share personal information with third parties for their own independent marketing purposes, other than the advertising cookie practices disclosed above.

8. Data Retention

We retain contact-form submissions only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and maintain reasonable business records, after which they are deleted or anonymized. Cookie-based data is retained according to the retention periods set by the relevant cookie or advertising vendor, which you can review in each vendor's own policy.

9. Your Privacy Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information, and to opt out of certain data sharing or personalized advertising. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 12. You can also manage advertising cookie preferences directly through the opt-out tools listed in Section 4, and manage general cookies through your browser settings.

10. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can promptly remove it.

11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information submitted through this site. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. The "Effective date" at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the site after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
