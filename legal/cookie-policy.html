Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which ones this site uses, and how you can control them.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function, to remember preferences, and to provide information to site owners and advertising partners.

2. Cookies we use

CategoryPurposeConsent required?
Strictly necessaryRemembering your cookie consent choice and basic site functionalityNo — always active

3. Your consent choices

When you first visit the site, a banner lets you accept all cookies or reject non-essential ones. Rejecting non-essential cookies disables analytics and personalized advertising cookies while keeping the site functional. You can change your choice at any time by clearing cookies for this site in your browser, which will show the banner again on your next visit.

4. Third-party cookies

Google AdSense may set cookies used to serve and measure advertising. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites. Learn more or opt out via Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info.

5. Managing cookies in your browser

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings menu. Blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of this and other websites.
